# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Potential clients (business owners, startups, entrepreneurs) looking for professional web development, SEO, e-commerce, and digital marketing services. They visit the site to evaluate SpaceCatWeb's expertise, see past work, understand pricing, and decide whether to contact the agency. The primary audience is Spanish-speaking (Peru/LATAM), with an English alternative available.

## Product Purpose

SpaceCatWeb is a web agency's own marketing site. It exists to showcase the agency's capabilities, build trust with prospects, and convert visitors into leads. Success is measured by contact form submissions and chat inquiries. The site itself must demonstrate the quality of work the agency delivers — it's their own portfolio piece.

## Brand Personality

- **Cosmic**: space theme, exploratory, expansive, forward-looking
- **Professional**: polished, reliable, expert confidence
- **Playful**: cat mascot, warm orange tones, approachable, memorable

Voice: confident but not arrogant, warm but not saccharine, technical but accessible. Like a friendly expert who explains complex things simply.

## Anti-references

- **Old SpaceCatWeb PHP site**: dated Bootstrap 4 look, generic template feel, no visual identity
- **Generic AI-generated landing pages**: cream/sand backgrounds, Fraunces/Inter/Playfair font stacks, gradient text, glassmorphism as default, numbered section markers (01/02/03), tiny uppercase tracked eyebrows above every section, identical card grids
- **Overly "tech bro" dark patterns**: cyberpunk aesthetics that sacrifice readability for coolness

## Design Principles

1. **Practice what you preach**: The site must feel like a premium web development product. Every animation, layout choice, and micro-interaction is a demonstration of capability.
2. **Show, don't tell**: Use motion, visual effects, and interactive elements to communicate skill rather than claiming it in text.
3. **One signature moment**: The hero (video + cat + canvas dissolve) is the memorable hook. Everything else supports quietly.
4. **Cosmic warmth**: Orange palette with dark backgrounds creates warmth without being generic. Space theme without being cliché.
5. **Expert restraint**: Bold where it counts, disciplined everywhere else. Not every section needs an animation or a special effect.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA compliance (contrast ≥4.5:1 body text, ≥3:1 large text)
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` — provide static alternatives for all animations
- Keyboard navigable throughout
- Semantic HTML with proper heading hierarchy
- Bilingual (ES/EN) with language switcher
